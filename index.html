What is CommandBridge?

CommandBridge is a personal lab for building sci-fi-inspired interfaces, automations, and widgets across Linux, Android, Home Assistant, and Meta smart glasses.

The projects and widgets here are real tools I use: message-driven control paths, dashboard experiments, and small surface UIs that feel like a ship's bridge.
